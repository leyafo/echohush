Your Safe Haven for Thoughts and Memories

Echohush is a minimalist journaling app designed to keep your thoughts secure, organized, and truly yours. Write with confidence, knowing your story stays private.

AES-256 ENCRYPTION

Your diary is locked with AES-256 encryption, ensuring absolute privacy. Your thoughts are yours alone—safe from prying eyes.

EFFORTLESS WRITING

Start writing painlessly with a clean, simple interface. Markdown support makes formatting a breeze, so you can focus on your words.

FULL-TEXT SEARCH

Find any entry in seconds. Search for any word or phrase, and Echohush will instantly bring up your past journals.

No ads, no distractions. Echohush is a pure, uninterrupted journaling experience—no forced content, ever.

FULL KEYBOARD SUPPORT

Edit comfortably without a mouse. Echohush is built for writers, with seamless keyboard navigation for a smooth journaling flow.

Open Source

Echohush is fully open-source, so you can trust there are no backdoors—your privacy is guaranteed. Rest easy knowing your journal is secure and transparent.
